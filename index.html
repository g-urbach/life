📖 How it works

Let’s Talk — Start a voice conversation. The AI asks you questions about your life and listens to your answers.

Typed Entry — Prefer to type? Write any memory or reflection here.

Voice Memo — Record a longer spoken memory in one go.

Photos — Upload a photo and tell the story behind it.

My Story — Generate a beautifully written memoir or funny roast. Export to PDF.

Timeline — Browse all your entries in chronological order.

🖨️ Printing your story

Export as PDF, then upload to a print-on-demand service:

  • Lulu.com — flexible, global shipping
  • Blurb.com — excellent photo book quality
  • Momento.com.au — Australian, high quality

🔒 Privacy & Terms

AI Processing. Story generation, duplicate detection and writing assistance sends your memoir content to Groq, Inc. Groq does not use API data to train their models.

Print Services. If you order a printed book, your story is sent to Peecho B.V. for fulfilment.

Data Storage. Your entries, photos and account data are stored securely in Supabase. No data is sold or shared beyond the above.

Data Care. We take reasonable care to protect your data but recommend exporting your entries periodically as a backup.

Liability. The developer shall not be liable for any loss or harm arising from your use of this application.

📶 You’re offline — changes won’t save until reconnected

⚠️ Delete My Data

This will permanently delete all your life story entries. This cannot be undone. Your account will remain active.
