🔒 Privacy & Terms

AI Processing. Story generation, duplicate detection and writing assistance sends your memoir content to Groq, Inc. Groq does not use API data to train their models.

Print Services. If you order a printed book, your story is sent to Peecho B.V. for fulfilment.

Data Storage. Your entries, photos and account data are stored securely in Supabase. No data is sold or shared beyond the above.

Data Care. We take reasonable care to protect your data but recommend exporting your entries periodically as a backup.

Liability. The developer shall not be liable for any loss or harm arising from your use of this application.

⚠️ Delete Account

Permanently deletes your account and all data — every entry, photo, and setting. This cannot be undone. Your email address will be released immediately.

📶 You’re offline — changes won’t save until reconnected

⚠️ Delete My Data

This will permanently delete all your life story entries. This cannot be undone. Your account will remain active.

Privacy & Terms — v1.1

LifeTold is a personal memoir companion that helps you record, organise and preserve your life story. By continuing you agree to the following:

1. Third-Party AI Processing. To generate stories, check for duplicate entries, and assist with writing, your memoir content is sent to Groq, Inc. for AI processing. Groq does not use API data to train their models. See Groq’s Privacy Policy.

2. Print Services. If you order a printed book, your story is sent to Peecho B.V. for print-on-demand fulfilment. See Peecho’s Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage. Your entries, photos and account data are stored securely in Supabase. See Supabase’s Privacy Policy.

4. Data Care. We take reasonable care to protect your data but cannot guarantee against loss in the event of a technical failure. We recommend exporting your entries periodically.

5. Limitation of Liability. The developer shall not be liable for any loss or harm arising from your use of this application.
